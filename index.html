Places to Visit

Embark on a journey with Lanka View to explore Sri Lanka's top destinations, from historic landmarks to pristine beaches and vibrant cities, offering a curated blend of cultural richness and natural wonders.

Sigiriya

Ascend the iconic Sigiriya Rock, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and marvel at ancient citadel ruins and panoramic vistas.

Galle

Wander through the charming streets of Galle, where colonial architecture and coastal beauty converge in a picturesque setting.

Udawalawe

Experience wildlife wonders in Udawalawe, a sanctuary where elephants roam freely against a backdrop of scenic landscapes.

Adams Peak

Ascend the revered Adams Peak, a pilgrimage site offering breathtaking views and spiritual significance.

Anuradhapura

Step into the sacred city of Anuradhapura, where ancient ruins and sacred sites narrate the tale of Sri Lanka's storied past.

Dambulla

Uncover ancient marvels in the historic cave temples of Dambulla, where rich history meets intricate rock art.
